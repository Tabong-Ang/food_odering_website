Featured Experiences

At City Grill, we bring the vibrant flavors of Africa to life with a menu designed to satisfy your cravings and delight your taste buds. Located in the heart of Kumba at Buea Road, beside Fomenky Street, our restaurant is the perfect spot to enjoy a hearty meal, whether you're dining in, ordering for delivery, or taking out.

Reserve a Table

Reserving your table is effortless and ensures that your visit is seamlessly tailored to your preferences. Our online reservation system allows you to select the date, time, and party size, providing you with the convenience of planning your visit at your leisure.

FOOD GALLERY

Enjoy dishes crafted with love, tradition, and the freshest ingredients. From sizzling grilled chicken and fish to crunchy plantain chips, fried rice, and juicy hamburgers, we have something for everyone.
